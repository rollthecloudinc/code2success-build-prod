The Code to Success LLC is a grassroots educational organization dedicated to transforming lives by providing underprivileged individuals with essential programming and technological skills. Our mission is to empower those who need it most, including incarcerated individuals, the homeless, recent migrants, overlooked veterans, and underprivileged children.

About Tune Bar Arm

Support

Tune is an innovative educational program by The Code to Success LLC, designed to provide underprivileged youth with foundational programming skills and modern web development techniques. Our mission is to empower 9th to 12th-grade students, offering them the tools they need to succeed in the tech industry. Our curriculum is designed to cover three integrated depths at every step.

Bar is an innovative educational program by The Code to Success LLC, designed to provide incarcerated individuals with foundational programming skills and modern web development techniques. Our mission is to empower these individuals, offering them the tools they need to reintegrate into society and secure meaningful employment. Our curriculum is designed to cover three integrated depths at every step.

Arm is an innovative educational program by The Code to Success LLC, designed to provide homeless individuals and recent migrants with foundational programming skills and modern web development techniques. Our mission is to empower these individuals, offering them the tools they need to integrate into the workforce and build stable, fulfilling lives. Our curriculum is designed to cover three integrated depths at every step.

Comprehensive Curriculum

  1. High-Demand Programming Languages: C, Java, Python, JavaScript, and TypeScript.
  2. Modern Full-Stack Web Development: Front-end and back-end development using frameworks like Angular and Node.js.
  3. Lifecycle & Sustainable Deployment: From planning to deployment, focusing on fiscally responsible and eco-friendly practices.
